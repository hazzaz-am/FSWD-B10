‘Genocide must be prosecuted, the killers will not be investigated’

Staff Reporter

(2 days ago) 22 August 2024, Wednesday, 8:17 p.m

Regarding the student-public movement, leaders of the Democracy Platform have stated that every bullet must be accounted for, the perpetrators of the genocide must be brought to justice, and those responsible for the killings should not be allowed to lead the investigation. They called for the formation of a special tribunal to ensure justice. These statements were made by the platform leaders during a press conference organized by the Democracy Platform at the central office of Citizen Unity on Wednesday. The Democracy Platform leaders held the press conference following their visit to Rangpur and Bogura, where they paid tribute to the martyr Abu Saeed at his grave, and met with his family, activists, and local people. The leaders emphasized the need to create a complete list of the martyrs and the injured, provide advanced medical treatment to the injured, and offer financial and necessary support to the families of the deceased and injured. They called for the return of Sheikh Hasina, the principal accused in cases of genocide, enforced disappearances, murders, looting, money laundering, and election fraud, to the country, where she and her accomplices should be tried. They also highlighted that Abu Saeed is a beacon of light whose sacrifice inspired people to overcome fear. The leaders noted that millions of students and members of the public across the country have become more vocal in their protests, accelerating the movement and forcing the invincible autocrat to flee. The government's primary responsibility at this moment, they asserted, is to compile a list of these brave soldiers who have contributed to freeing the country from fascism. The leaders further demanded the establishment of a special tribunal to try those involved in the July genocide, from Sheikh Hasina down to her accomplices and members of the various forces who participated in the massacre. The families of the martyrs must be taken care of, and the injured must receive proper medical treatment either within the country or abroad without delay. Additionally, the assets of business groups and families involved in bank fraud, corruption, and embezzlement must be confiscated in favor of the state, and the laundered money must be recovered. The Democracy Platform leaders also stressed that the main spirit of the uprising is to reform the current fascist, autocratic state centered around one individual and to establish an accountable, just, and equitable state and society. To achieve this goal, the interim government must initiate discussions on state reforms with representatives from the political parties, civil society, and all sections of society. The press conference was attended by Mahmudur Rahman Manna, President of Citizen Unity; Saiful Haque, General Secretary of the Revolutionary Workers Party; Zonayed Saki, Chief Coordinator of the People's Solidarity Movement; Sheikh Rafiqul Islam Bablu, Convener of the Followers of Bhashani Council; Md. Siraj Mia, Senior Vice-President of the Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal (JSD); and Imran Iman, Organizational Coordinator of the State Reform Movement.
